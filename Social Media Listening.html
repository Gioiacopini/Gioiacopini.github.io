First look at the data¶

On average

  • 0.062% of FB page fans like a post
  • 0.013% of fans share a post
  • 0.006% of fans comment a post
  • 0.082% of people that were exposed to the post interacted with it (liked, shared or commented)

  • a post stays active for 7.8 days regardless of the media contained in them

  • posts containing videos reach the most people and are more liked, commented and shared
